about

As a first-year MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions) student, I am passionate about the digital world, new technologies, and tools that transform our daily lives. Through this project on the evolution of the credit card, I aim to explore how technological advancements are reshaping our financial behavior and redefining our relationship with money.

My approach to this project is both informative and critical, combining a historical analysis of the credit card with cultural works that question our relationship to money and power, such as the movie "In Time," the book "The End of Money," or films by David Fincher. My goal is to offer a comprehensive and accessible perspective, for both experts and enthusiasts, to understand the impact of these changes on our current and future society.
